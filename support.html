Frequently Asked Questions
  Add Time
  1. Go to the "Time" tab.
  2. Tap the "Add Time" button.
  3. Enter the date and time and time.
  4. Tap "Save."
  Stopwatch

Start Stopwatch

  1. Go to the "Time" tab.
  2. Tap the "Stopwatch" button.
  3. Tap "Start Time."

Stop Stopwatch

  1. Go to the "Time" tab.
  2. Tap the "Stopwatch" button.
  3. Tap "Stop Time."
    • To save the time tap "Yes."
    • To discard the time tap "No"
    • To cancel tap "Cancel."
  Add Call
  1. Go to the "Calls" tab.
  2. Tap the "Map" button.
  3. Long-press on the location want to add the call. A circle will appear marking the location of the new call.
  4. By default, the call is marked as not-at-home. To update the call status,
    1. Tap the call once.
    2. Tap the "Status" option.
    3. Select the new status.

    Note: More information and options can be obtanied by tapping the "Info" button.

  Share List of Not-At-Homes
  • Go to the "Calls" tab.
  • Tap the "dots" button.
  • Select the "Share Calls" option.
  • Select the types of calls you want to share.
  • By default, the call is marked as not-at-home. To update the call status,
    1. Tap the call once.
    2. Tap the "Status" option.
    3. Select the new status.

    Note: More information and options can be obtanied by tapping the "Info" button.
