Frequently Asked Questions
  Add Shopping List

Add Existing List

  1. Open Peppercorn.
  2. Go to the "Shopping Lists" tab.
  3. Tap the Plus button.
  4. Choose "Choose from Reminders."
  5. Tap the list you want to add.

Create New List

  1. Open Peppercorn.
  2. Go to the "Shopping Lists" tab.
  3. Tap the Plus button.
  4. Choose "New List."
  Sharing Lists
  1. Open the Reminders app.
  2. Tap the list you want to share.
  3. Tap the Share Button.
  Import Recipe from Web
  1. Copy the URL of the recipe you want to import.
  2. Open Peppercorn.
  3. Go to the "Recipes" tab.
  4. Tap the Plus button.
  5. Paste the URL in the "Recipe URL" text field.
  6. Tap "Import Recipe."
  7. Tap "Save."
  Plan Meals
  1. Open Peppercorn.
  2. Go to the "Recipes" tab.
  3. Tap the "Meal Plan" button.
  4. Tap "Edit."
  5. Tap the "Add Meal" button.
  6. Choose the meals you want to add.
  7. Tap "Done."
  Inventory Item Options

Barcode

  • Tap the "Scan Barcode" button to scan the item's barcode.
  • You can tap the barcode button in the search bar to scan an item's barcode, making it easy to find it later.
  • All of the most common barcodes are supported.

Note: Turn on the "Reorder" switch to access the following options.

Reorder Period

  • Set how often you want the item to be automatically added to your list.

List

  • Choose the list you want the item to be added to when the reorder time elapses.

Category

  • Choose what category to add the item to in the specified list.

Reorder Notification

  • Choose whether or not to recieve a notification when the item was automatically added to your list.
